How to Play 🪱

1 Read the answer clue at the top of the board.
2 Drag across any letters to trace out a word — all words will begin/end near adjacent words in the sentence.
3 Found words appear as chips in the bar below. Drag them to rearrange into the correct sentence.
4 The number in brackets tells you how many words are in the sentence.
Example
Clue: Avocado (6)
Answer: Creamy Fruit Required To Make Guacamole
5 Use Undo to release the last word, or drag a chip onto Undo to remove any word.
