How to Play 🪱

1 Read the answer clue at the top of the board. Your goal is to create a sentence describing the answer.
Example
Answer: Robin
Question: Sidekick to the superhero Batman
2 Drag across letters to trace out words — words can snake in any cardinal direction across the grid, including backwards and upwards.
3 Selected words appear in the section below the grid. Drag them around to rearrange the sentence into the correct order. All words will begin/end near adjacent words in the grid.
4 Tap or Drag words to 🗑️ to remove any words from the sentence.
5 Tap the Hints button if you need a nudge in the right direction.
