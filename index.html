How to Play 🪱

1 Read the answer clue at the top of the board. Your goal is to create a sentence describing the answer.
Example
Answer: Robin
Question: Sidekick to the superhero Batman
2 Drag across letters to trace out words — words can snake in any cardinal direction across the grid, including backwards and upwards.
3 Selected words appear in the section below the grid. Drag them around to rearrange the sentence into the correct order.
4 Neighboring words in the sentence will have touching first/last letters in the grid.
5 Tap or Drag words to 🗑️ to remove any words from the sentence.
6 Tap the Hints button if you need a nudge in the right direction.

🪱 Semicolon Sunday!

Today's puzzle is a Semicolon Sunday, which signifies the end/beginning of a new week! The answer sentence contains two separate groups of words describing the clue.

Example
Answer: Robin;
Question: red breasted songbird sidekick to the superhero batman

Reminder: Your rearranged sentence will still follow the same everyday rules — neighboring words in the sentence will have touching first/last letters in the grid.
